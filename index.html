ZERO HUNGER



What is zero hunger?

Zero hunger is the secind of the 17 sustainable development goals eastablished by the United Nations in 2015.

The goal for zero hunger is:

"End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition and promote sustainable". In essence, zero hunger aims to ensure that everyone, everywhere , has access to sufficient, safe and nutrious food all year round.

Key components and targets of this campaign

  • Ending hunger; making sure that no one suffers from lack of food.
  • Ending all forms of malnutrition; addressing undernutrition like stunting and wasting in children, as well as hidden hunger and overweight/obesity.
  • Achieving food security; ensuring that all people have physical, social, and economic access to adequate food.
